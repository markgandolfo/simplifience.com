Simplifience

noun
  1. Simplified science
  2. Science, less the accumulated cruft of history

Behold the deepest question ever asked by a conscious mind: "Why?"

Math and science are our attempts at answering this unanswerable question. Unfortunately, they are often taught poorly.

Science is often coupled with its history, dragging students through unnecessary periods of confusion.

Mathematics is often taught by memorization, hiding the deep intrinsic beauties.

Simplifience does neither. It dives straight to the root of the issue. If you want to really grok math and science, start here.
